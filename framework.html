WINBY FRAMEWORK

Decision Mediation in AI-Driven Evaluation Systems

winby is an AI decision mediation layer for agent-driven evaluation.

This can be understood as a structured approach to how AI systems evaluate and choose between alternatives.

It introduces a structured layer between AI evaluation and final selection, focusing on how alternatives are interpreted, compared, and prioritized.

1. Structural Shift

Digital systems have historically relied on human attention, visibility, and manual comparison. As AI systems increasingly take over discovery and evaluation, decision-making shifts away from browsing toward structured, automated comparison processes.

In this environment, influence is no longer defined primarily by what is seen, but by how evaluation is conducted.

2. System Overview

winby introduces a mediation layer between AI evaluation and final decision.

User Intent → AI Evaluation → Mediation Layer → Decision Outcome

The system focuses on structuring how AI systems interpret, compare, and prioritize options rather than competing for visibility.

3. Core Capabilities

Structured Comparison

Defines structured comparison workflows so alternatives can be evaluated consistently rather than informally inferred.

Evaluation Sequencing

Organizes evaluation criteria and sequencing, shaping how options are assessed within AI-driven workflows.

Traceable Decisions

Enables traceable decision pathways, making evaluation processes more inspectable and reproducible.

4. What Is Being Explored

winby is currently being developed and tested in controlled prototype environments.

The current work focuses on structuring reproducible evaluation processes, where comparison steps, criteria ordering, and decision logic are explicitly defined. This enables consistent and inspectable evaluation pathways within AI-driven systems.

At this stage, the emphasis is on process-level structure rather than outcome optimization.

5. Implications

This approach has implications across domains where AI assists or replaces human comparison, including procurement, recommendation systems, and automated evaluation workflows.

As evaluation becomes increasingly automated, structuring decision processes becomes the primary point of influence.

6. Related Work

Related conceptual work explores broader system-level transitions in AI-driven environments, including agent-based evaluation processes and shifts from visibility-based to system-level influence mechanisms.

winby represents an early-stage step toward formalizing decision mediation as a foundational layer in AI-driven systems.
