AI Decision Mediation System

A system that reshapes how AI agents evaluate and make decisions beyond traditional click-based pathways.

User Intent → AI Agent Evaluation → winby Mediation Layer → Decision Outcome

Problem

Digital systems today rely on human-driven browsing, clicking, and comparison. As AI agents increasingly replace users in discovery and evaluation, traditional click-based models lose relevance.

System

winby introduces a mediation layer within AI-driven decision flows. Instead of influencing visibility, the system intervenes in how AI agents interpret, compare, and select outcomes.

Early Validation

The system has been tested in early-stage real-world scenarios, where observable shifts in decision pathways occurred when mediated through winby logic.

Implication

This reflects a structural transition from click-based interaction to AI-driven decision systems, where influence moves from attention to evaluation.
